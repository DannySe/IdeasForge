Organize your ideas thoughts tasks

Neuronizer offers the opportunity to order your thoughts, ideas and tasks in categories to recognize the information you need as fast as possible.

Designed to be fast

Save your ideas within a few seconds. Recognize the information you need instantly. Neuronizer is designed to be usable in every situation.

User Experience Focused

Use the newest features and design offered by Android. Find out all the possibilities Neuronizer offers you in under a minute.

Keeps your data private

All of your data is stored locally on your phone in a secure environment. You are the only one who is capable to read your data.
