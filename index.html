Your place to be creative get noticed shape future

IdeasForge is a space which connects outstanding ideas and ressources for their realization. We help you shape your future today.

1
Pitch your idea

Show us what you and your fellow students are working on. Bring your projects to IdeasForge and share your passion for innovative ideas with like-minded people.

2
Convince the jury

Win the jury's hearts with an inspiring vision, convincing arguments and an exceptional presentation. Become one of the finalists and get a chance to launch your startup.

3
Enjoy the benifits

Get support from well-known companies and partner organisations. From assistance in creating a business plan to free office space - everything is possible!

Who can take part?

Any student at DHBW-Karlsruhe is welcome to take part. If you are working on an innovative project in one of your courses at DH or develop an exciting idea in your free time - you are exactly what we are looking for!

Where can I apply?

You can get recommended by a Professer in charge of a DH-course which your project is a part of, or just register yourself. All you need is a presentation or a short video showcasing your product, which will convince the jury that your idea is startup-worthy.

How are the winners picked?

The jury consists of former DH-Students and young entrepreneurs. They will decide which projects have the highest potential and should get supported by our partners. The audience will have a chance to influence the decision and save their favorite if it did not get picked by the jury.
